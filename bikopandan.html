BIKO PANDAN RECIPE

DESRCIPTION

Pandan Biko Recipe is perfect for fiestas or whenever you are just
craving for some authentic Filipino meryenda. Whether you are cooking
this for yourself or for a group, its taste will stick in your mind for
a while. Indeed, adding Pandan is a great variation to this kakanin that we all love.

INGREDIENTS

- 1/2 sticky rice or malagkit
- 2 cups coconut milk
- 3/2 cups sugar
- 1 tsp pandan green powder
- 1 tsp salt
- 1 tsp vanilla

For toppings :
- 2 cups coconut milk for latik

PROCEDURE

1. Cook the glutinous rice with coconut milk
2. Set aside, and let's make for the latik.
3. Stir coconut milk until becomes golden brown latik.
4. Set aside, pour another coconut milk, and add pandan powder, vanilla, salt, and sugar.
5. Add the cooked glutinous rice, and mix all.
6. Mix until becomes smooth and sticky.
7. Serve in plate with banana leaves and flatten it.
8. Slice in pieces, then add latik for toppings.
9. Ready to serve, enjoy your biko pandan!

FACTS

Biko pandan is a rich, chewy Filipino rice cake made with sticky
rice, coconut milk, and dark sugar. Traditionally served in a round,
shallow bamboo tray lined with banana leaves known as a bilao, biko belongs
to a category of sweets called kakanin, which is made up entirely of rice cakes.
Try this savory sweet delicacy that you will surely love, as well as your family.
