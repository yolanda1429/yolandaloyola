PUTO BANANA CHEESE RECIPE

DESCRIPTION

Banana Cheese Puto are little steamed cakes topped with cheese and flavored with
banana. It can be served with butter or grated fresh coconut on the side and can be
enjoyed anytime of the day.

INGREDIENTS

- 4 Bananas
- 2 cups All-Purpose flour
- 1 cup Sugar
- 1 1/4 tablespoons Baking powder
- 1 cup Water
- 1 cup Evaporated milk
- 1 medium egg
- 1/4 cup Unsalted butter
- 36 pieces Cheddar cheese

PROCEDURE

In a mixing bowl, mash 4 bananas.
1. In separate bowl, mix all remaining ingredients, except for flour.
2. Add flour gradually and mix all.
3. After that, ready the puto molders.
4. Brush puto molders with margarine (butter for susbtitution), for not the puto will glued into puto molder.
5. Fill each molder and place in steamer, and add sliced cheese for toppings.
6. Steam for 10-15 minutes. After that, ready to serve. Enjoy your banana puto cheese!

FACTS

These Banana Puto are easier than easy to make. Its fluffy,
soft and healthy for your kids too. The potassium and magnesium in
bananas can help lower blood pressure. Its magnesium can also help us
develop strong bones.
